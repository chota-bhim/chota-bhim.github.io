An Adventure On Time Travel

One day while playing table tennis with my friends in my friend's house, the ball of the table tennis went inside the basement. We all went down to fetch the ball and discovered a big metallic structure with a big cover which looked very dusty. I asked my friend what it was, as it was his house. But then he said that he didn't know and said that it was not his and it was not there before. We all went up and asked his parents if they had bought it. But they all said no. They too went down to check but told that they didn't know.

We pulled down the big cover. It was even dusty inside. We started dusting the machine with a broom. We found some buttons but still could not understand what it was. We found a control panel on the machine. We also found papers, and a remote lying around. The paper looked like the manual of the thing. On reading the manual we understood how it and the remote worked. We all exclaimed as we had found a Time Travel Machine . The remote was used to come back if we would go anywhere. The machine would take us anywhere in the past and also in the future. It had many working systems. We were all very excited.

I said to my friends that I would go to the future and check when the earth would be destroyed and how. My friends said that they would go to Delhi sultanate free of cost and just in a few seconds. We all agreed with each other with all the plans. Some of our friends said that they could not go if their parents denied it. I said that I would talk to their parents about it. Then their parents hesitated but agreed when we allowed their parents to come with us. we all packed the bags and stood ready to go.

We first decided to go to the Mughal Period. I took my luggage and my friend took the remote of the machine. I also had an electric drone with me to sell and get some identical coins at that time. We typed "Mughal Period after Shah Jahan" on the machine and a big black like structure pulled us in. We were all afraid for some time but calmed reaching the place. We could understand that the machine worked properly as it was the "Mughal Period" as people wore clothes of that time and the shops were very different.

We walked some distance and found the market. I thought of selling the drone that I had bought with me. I sold it and got some silver coins. I also told them how it worked. They were very surprised. the people there asked about us but we somehow escaped those questions. We wanted to stay there for a long time but due to our father's work and our schools, we had to come back. The rupia was different from ours. We went to a shop and ate roti sabji found there. We came back home when nobody was there.

From then on I always like to travel in time either in the past or future. I liked the people there and the way they lived. I also liked the clean and tidy place.
